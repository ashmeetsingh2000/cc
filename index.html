Meet Your Instructor - Chetali Chadha
Content Creator
Chetali is a highly regarded content creator in India, known for her deep knowledge of skincare, beauty, and lifestyle. Driven by her innate passion, she consistently delivers valuable content in various formats to her 675,000+ subscribers on YouTube and Instagram.
Brand Consultant
Drawing on her extensive expertise in ingredients, formulations, applications, and results, Chetali has provided guidance to numerous leading skincare and beauty brands in creating effective products for Indian skin. Her recommendations, which range from fragrance-free options to potent hero ingredients, have been instrumental in representing the needs and preferences of Indian users to major skincare companies.
Entrepreneur & Corporate Executive
Before content creation, Chetali spent a decade in the Corporate World and developed core management and people skills in several companies - Amex, NDTV Good Times, Emaar MGF, Jabong, Myntra, Medlife. She has also tried her hands at entrepreneurship in the area of offline Beauty and Lifestyle exhibitions.
