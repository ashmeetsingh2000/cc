Should I conduct a patch test before trying any of the products recommended in this course?

All views, opinions and suggestions expressed in this skincare course regarding any of the products, are based on Chetali's personal experience with such products (as well as that of her close family, friends and colleagues). These views, opinions and suggestions for such products may or may not work for you. This is because Skincare and Haircare is subjective. What works for one person's skin / hair, may or may not work for another.

Therefore, always conduct a PATCH TEST before including any new products in your skincare, haircare, make-up routine.

Are the skincare course's recommendations a substitute for medical advice from a dermatologist or medical specialist?

The product recommendations discussed in this course are NOT meant to be a medical recommendation. Therefore, for any medical conditions you SHOULD consult your Dermatologist or Medical Specialist. This course is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice.
