Privacy Policy
At chetalichadha.com ("Website"), we value and prioritize the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase of our online courses. By accessing or using our website, you consent to the terms outlined in this policy.

A. Information We Collect

A.1. Personal Information

When you register an account, make a purchase, or communicate with us, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, contact details, and billing information. This information is necessary to process your transactions and provide you with the requested services.

A.2. Usage Data

We may collect non-personal information about your interactions with our website, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referral source, and pages visited. This data helps us improve our website's functionality and user experience.

B. Use of Collected Information

B.1. Provision of Services

We use your personal information to process your course orders, deliver the courses, and communicate with you regarding your purchases. We may also send you relevant updates, notifications, and promotional offers related to our courses and services.

B.2. Personalization

To enhance your experience, we may use your information to personalize the content, recommendations, and any advertisements displayed on our website. This ensures that the courses and resources we offer align with your interests and preferences.

B.3. Customer Support

Your information enables us to respond to your inquiries, provide technical assistance, and address any concerns you may have. We strive to deliver timely and effective customer support to ensure your satisfaction.

B.4 Analytics and Improvements

We utilize collected data for analytical purposes to gain insights into user behaviour, trends, and preferences. This information helps us improve our website, courses, and overall service offerings. All data is anonymized and aggregated to protect your privacy.

C. Information Sharing

C.1. Third-Party Service Providers

We may engage trusted third-party service providers to facilitate certain aspects of our business operations. These may include Learning Management Systems, Hosting Services, Payment Processors, Marketing Platforms, or Customer Support Tools. These providers have access to your personal information solely for performing the assigned tasks and are obligated to maintain its confidentiality.

C.2. Legal Requirements

We may disclose your personal information if required by law, court order, or government regulation to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others.

C.3. Consent

Except as outlined in this policy, we will not share, sell, rent, or disclose your personal information to any third party without your explicit consent.

D. Data Security

D.1. User Responsibility

While we strive to protect your personal information, it is essential for you to take precautions to protect your account credentials and any sensitive information you provide. Do not share your login details with others and use strong passwords.

E. Links to External Websites

Our website may contain links to external websites or resources that are not operated by us. Please note that we have no control over the content, practices, or privacy policies of these third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any websites you visit outside of our domain.

Our website and services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with their personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to remove the information from our systems.

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically. Your continued use of our website after any modifications indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
